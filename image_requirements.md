# Image Asset Requirements for Kichen Kumfurt

To bring the website to life and replace the dark wireframe placeholders, you will need to gather or shoot the following high-quality images. Since this is a luxury brand, **all images should be high-resolution, professionally lit, and ideally have a slightly dark, moody, or cinematic tone** to match the midnight indigo (`#0A0E17`) aesthetic of the website.

---

## 1. Global Brand Assets

### **Main Logo**
* **Filename**: `main_logo.png`
* **Format**: PNG with a transparent background.
* **Description**: The primary brand logo or icon. It should be light/white colored so it is visible against the dark header. 
* **Location**: Top left of the navigation bar.

---

## 2. Homepage Images

### **Hero Background Image**
* **Orientation**: Landscape (Wide) - 16:9 or ultra-wide.
* **Description**: The very first thing the user sees at the top of the homepage. This needs to be a breathtaking, wide-angle shot of a stunning, modern luxury kitchen. 
* **Style**: Cinematic lighting, showing off sleek countertops, premium lighting fixtures, and clean architectural lines.

### **Craftsmanship / Architecture Image**
* **Orientation**: Portrait (Vertical) - 4:5 or 9:16.
* **Description**: This goes in the "Uncompromising Craftsmanship" section next to the text. 
* **Style**: An elegant detail shot. Think a close-up of a seamless edge-banded cabinet, German hardware in action, or a beautiful marble/quartz island with soft lighting. 

### **Bespoke Living Collections (4 Images)**
These are the image cards in the grid. They should ideally have a cohesive look (e.g., all shot with similar lighting).
* **Orientation**: Standard Landscape (4:3 ratio).
* **Images Needed**:
  1. **Luxury Kitchens**: A beautiful, wide shot of a completed modular kitchen.
  2. **Bespoke Wardrobes**: An elegant walk-in closet or sliding glass wardrobe with warm internal lighting.
  3. **Storage Solutions**: A smart, organized pull-out pantry, magic corner, or intelligent drawer system.
  4. **Premium Materials**: A beautiful flat-lay or angled shot of material swatches (wood, acrylic, stone) or a close-up of a premium finish.

---

## 3. About Page Images

### **Founder's Portrait / Studio Image**
* **Orientation**: Portrait (Vertical) - 4:5 ratio.
* **Description**: This sits next to "The Founder's Vision". 
* **Style**: A professional, moody, high-end portrait of the principal designer (Aashish Jajoo) in a bespoke kitchen setting, OR a highly aesthetic shot of a master craftsman working on a premium material in the studio.

---

## 4. Portfolio & Materials Pages (Future Content)

### **Portfolio Gallery**
* **Orientation**: Mixed (Square, Landscape, Portrait).
* **Description**: A collection of 6 to 12 of your best completed projects. You want a mix of wide shots (entire kitchens) and detail shots (handles, lighting, countertops).

### **Material Textures**
* **Orientation**: Square (1:1).
* **Description**: If you plan to expand the Materials page, you will want seamless texture squares of your finishes (High-gloss acrylics, PU finishes, Quartz grains, Natural wood veneers) so clients can view them digitally.

> [!TIP]
> **Performance Tip:** Before uploading the final images to the website, make sure to compress them (using a tool like TinyPNG or WebP conversion) so the website continues to load lightning-fast!
